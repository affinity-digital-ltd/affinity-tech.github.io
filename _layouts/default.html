Our Philosophy

We at affinity tech believe that the root of happiness is developed through sharing and kindness.

Therefore, we offer our services for free to organisations whose aim is to support communities with similar aims and approaches.

Our Mission

Our mission is to collaborate and cooperate with NPOs looking to provide a good cause. We would like to aid you in any way we can in delivering your mission and vision.

We offer the following services:
  • Web Apps
  • Websites
  • Web Design
  • Support for Hosting

Our Process

If you have a project which you believe affinity tech could help with, then please get in contact with us.

Please provide us with the following information:
  • What product/service you wish to provide
  • How others can benefit
  • How you would like us to support you
  • We aim to respond within 24 hours

Keep Up to Date

If you would like to follow updates about our new and existing projects or to discuss a project of your own. Then please feel free to sign up to our newsletter or contact us.
