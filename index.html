We feel that giving to the community is important. Which is why we offer our services for free to not-for-profit organisations.

Our Philosophy

We at affinity tech believe that the root of happiness is developed through sharing and kindness.

Therefore, we offer our services for free to organisations whose aim is to support communities with similar aims and approaches.

Our Mission

Our mission is to collaborate and cooperate with NPOs looking to provide a good cause. We would like to aid you in any way we can in delivering your mission and vision.

We offer the following services:
  • Web Apps
  • Websites
  • Web Design
  • Support for Hosting

Our Process

If you have a project which you believe affinity tech could help with, then please get in contact with your proposal.
